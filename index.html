About Sarika

Sarika is a designer and digital storyteller with 4 years of experience working on both technical and artistic teams, resolving design issues and working within tight deadlines. She has worked as a designer for the PBS Show "Peg + Cat" and for a Sesame Studios music video, and as an animator for Amazon's "Click Clack Moo: Christmas at the Farm". Through Rutgers Coding Bootcamp, Sarika honed her knowledge of HTML, CSS, Javascript as well as UI Design.

Each experience has taught her the art of bringing a director's vision to life, new techniques, bringing joy to people through her work.
